I'm thrilled to share that I've started working on **OpenLeaf**, a mobile application dedicated to bringing the power of free ebooks to readers everywhere.

## What is OpenLeaf App?

OpenLeaf is a mobile app built to provide a clean, distraction-free reading experience for thousands of free ebooks available through the [Project Gutenberg library](https://www.gutenberg.org/). It's designed for readers who want quality literature without the clutter, ads, or distractions of modern digital platforms.

## Timeline

The app will be ready in a few months and will launch first on the **Google Play Store** for Android users. This initial release will help us understand how the reading community engages with the app and what features matter most to you.

## iOS Coming Next

Based on the response and interest from our reading community, I will then begin work on an iOS version of the app. Your feedback during the Android launch will directly shape the iOS development roadmap.

## Stay Tuned

This is just the beginning of the OpenLeaf journey. Keep an eye on this space as we move toward launch, and thank you for your support in building a better reading experience for the world.
